Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

At Termify app, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, stored, and protected when using our application. Within this policy, "we," "our," or "app" refers to the Termify app and its developers. By using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it. This policy applies to all users of the Termify application, regardless of how you access or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

The Termify app is designed to minimize data collection while providing an excellent user experience. We collect only the information necessary to operate and improve our services:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device identifiers, and hardware specifications
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, session frequency, and interaction patterns
  • Learning Progress: Your study sessions, completed lessons, quiz results, and learning preferences
  • User-Generated Content: Term collections, favorites, custom notes, and personal study materials you create
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, loading times, and technical performance metrics
  • Settings and Preferences: Language settings, notification preferences, and app configuration choices

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our app, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns. This includes technical information such as your device's operating system, app version, and general usage statistics that help us understand how our app is performing.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as your real name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it for support purposes. We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for legitimate business purposes to provide and improve our services:

3.1 Service Provision

  • Delivering personalized learning experiences tailored to your progress and preferences
  • Maintaining your learning progress and study materials across app sessions
  • Providing relevant content recommendations based on your learning patterns
  • Enabling app features and functionality that enhance your learning experience

3.2 App Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to identify and fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understanding user behavior to develop new features and improvements
  • Optimizing app performance and user interface design
  • Conducting research and analytics to enhance educational effectiveness

3.3 Technical Operations

  • Monitoring app stability and performance across different devices
  • Detecting and preventing technical problems and security issues
  • Ensuring compatibility with various operating system versions
  • Maintaining and improving our technical infrastructure

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your personal learning data, including term collections, favorites, progress, and preferences, are primarily stored locally on your device. This approach ensures that your personal study materials remain under your direct control and are not transmitted to external servers without your explicit consent.

4.2 Cloud Synchronization

Optionally, you may choose to use Apple iCloud to synchronize your data across multiple devices. When you enable iCloud sync, your learning data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is subject to Apple's privacy policy and security measures. We do not have access to your iCloud data, and you can disable this feature at any time.

4.3 Data Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data both in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure coding practices and regular security audits

4.4 Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually remove it. Anonymous usage statistics may be retained for analytical purposes but cannot be linked back to individual users.

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for commercial purposes. Your privacy is not a commodity, and we are committed to keeping your personal data secure and private.

5.2 Anonymous Data Sharing

We may share anonymous, aggregated usage statistics and crash reports for app improvement purposes. This data is completely anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users. Such data helps us understand general usage patterns and improve the app for all users.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or if we believe such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others. We will make reasonable efforts to notify you of such disclosures unless prohibited by law.

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction. We will provide notice and ensure that the receiving party agrees to protect your information according to this privacy policy.

6. Third-Party Services and Integrations

6.1 Apple Services

Our app may utilize services provided by Apple, including analytics, crash reporting, and iCloud synchronization. These services are governed by Apple's privacy policy and terms of service. We recommend reviewing Apple's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

6.2 Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics services to help us understand app usage and improve our services. These services collect anonymous usage data and are configured to respect user privacy. We ensure that any third-party services we use maintain high privacy and security standards.

6.3 No Advertising Networks

We do not integrate with advertising networks or share your data with advertising companies. Our app is designed to provide a clean, ad-free learning experience without compromising your privacy.

7. Children's Privacy Protection

Protecting children's privacy is especially important to us. Our app does not target children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

7.1 Parental Guidance

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information from our systems and ensure compliance with applicable children's privacy laws.

7.2 Age Verification

We do not require users to provide their age or date of birth. However, if we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information.

7.3 Educational Use

While our app may be used in educational settings, we recommend that schools and educational institutions review this privacy policy and ensure compliance with applicable educational privacy laws such as FERPA and COPPA.

8. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Control

You have various rights regarding your personal data and how it is processed:

  • Right to Access: You can request information about what personal data we have about you
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Deletion: You can request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restriction: You can request limitation of how we process your data
  • Right to Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: You can object to certain types of data processing

8.2 Data Management

Since most of your data is stored locally on your device, you have direct control over it. You can delete your learning progress, collections, and preferences directly within the app settings. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

8.3 Opting Out

You can opt out of anonymous usage analytics and crash reporting through the app settings. However, this may limit our ability to improve the app and provide technical support.

9.2 Data Processing Locations

Your data may be processed in the United States, European Union, or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure that all processing locations maintain adequate data protection standards.
